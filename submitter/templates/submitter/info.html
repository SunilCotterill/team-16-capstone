Thank you for choosing our platform for your subletting needs! We are here to streamline the process of finding a compatible roommate.

What is ApartMate?

ApartMate is a new web app focused on helping the students of Waterloo find the best sublet they can. As uWaterloo students ourselves, we understand that the subletting process can be very demanding and time consuming. Our focus is to streamline the subletting process and ensure that you have the best opportunity for a real connection.

How Do We Help?

We have hand crafted a questionnaire based on what our users deem to be the most important things about living together. You can create a listing and we will provide a shareable link for you to put on your sublet posting, wherever you choose to do so. Potential sublets will fill out this questionnaire and then you can view and filter all the submissions from directly within our app. You can even shortlist the ones you like!

Where Do You Start?

You're already registered and logged in! Once you navigate to the home page you can create a listing by clicking on the "New Listing" button or click here. Further instructions are provided throughout our app!
